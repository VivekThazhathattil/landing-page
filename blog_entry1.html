Option Chain

Written by Stockzo Admin Friday, August 05, 2022

An option chain also known as an option matrix can be defined as a chart of useful data that helps the investors to differentiate between all stock contracts available for NIFTY stock and to help them predict the low and high levels of liquidity and price directions, it gives data about current security levels, valuation, and how they will influence in the long run. Clearly, we can say that the options chain has all the options of the available contracts. It has two different sections call and put.

Call option means that we get the right option of contract but are not bound to buy that elementary asset at a given rate and within the options expiration date.

Whereas, the put option means that we get the right option of contract but are not bound to sell that elementary asset at a given rate and within the options expiration date.

The point at which the investor becomes ready of buying a particular stock is known as an option strike.
